Cinnamon Raisin Bread

Prep time / cook time / total time

Serving size

Course [Salad]

Cuisine

Ingredient list

Dough

  • 220g warm water
  • 90g buttermilk
  • 5g yeast
  • 65g sugar
  • 525g AP flour
  • 14g salt
  • 130 melted butter

Filling

  • 10g cinnamon
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 2g salt
  • 150g raisin

Instructions

  1. Stir all ingredients using a spoon (3 minutes low speed, 4 minutes high speed using mixer)
  2. 30 minutes later, stretch and fold using hand
  3. 30 minutes later, stretch and fold again
  4. 90 minutes later
  5. Grease a bread pan
  6. Roll it to 12 x 16 rectangle
  7. Brush with egg wash
  8. Spread cinnamon, brown sugar and salt mix
  9. Then add raisin
  10. Roll, cut, twist
  11. Put in the pan, wait for 1 hour
  12. Preheat oven to 400F
  13. Brush egg wash on the bread
  14. Bake 30 minutes
  15. Cover will tin foil
  16. Bake at 350F at 15-20 minutes
  17. Enjoy.
